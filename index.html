ABOUT US

Our e-courses allow African artisans to connect directly with the consumer and accurately educate them on their culture and the story behind the product, in addition to how to make the product.

Our starter kits contain the tools and supplies essential to making authentic handmade African products. This gives the consumer the flexibility to also make design modifications.

All the African artisan has to do is produce the e-course and send us a list of raw materials required to make the handicraft. This allows us to bypass all the infrastructural issues suppliers face. And because we take control of distribution, we can eliminate the trust issues buyers have with sourcing from Africa.
